About Me

I come from Shengana Gewog, Punakha, and my parents live in Datagoenpa, making their livelihood through farming. I am the youngest of three siblings, with two elder sisters. Calm and composed by nature, I am also friendly and approachable, with strong communication skills that allow me to connect easily with others
